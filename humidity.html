Humidity

This plot shows the relationship between latitude and humidity of selected cities. It visualizes the trend of humidity changes along the latitude. There is no strong correlation between humidity and latitude.

While there is no strong direct correlation between humidity and latitude, there is a direct correlation between humidity and temperature as latitude approaching the equator. Locations closest to the equator have higher temperatures as well as higher humidity levels.
